**The Growth Engine Is Just Getting Warmed Up**
Look beyond the product cycle noise — Apple’s revenue grew 16.6% year-over-year, and earnings popped 21.8%. That’s not a company hitting a ceiling; that’s a $4.5 trillion titan accelerating.
